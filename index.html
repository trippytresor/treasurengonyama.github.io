BENT

BENT is a brand inspired by the early days of animation, following the topic of Silence is Golden, Rubber Hose animation was the first animation style that became standardized in the American animation industry. The defining feature of the style is arms and legs that are typically simple, flowing curves, without articulation - limbs that bend. The stories of the time made heavy use of the comedic style called slapstick, this is characterised by the use of physical gags to communicate. BENT takes these elements and elevates them to a new level. This is a brand that finds the simplest and toughest solution to creating the strongest board possible, a brand that can bend but will never break.
